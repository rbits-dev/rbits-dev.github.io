ABOUT RBITS
RBITS, an innovative liquidity-generation protocol, consistently rewards holders with token distributions. Gain exclusive benefits such as limited-edition NFTs through the Rabbit Boxes NFT Launchpad, unique in-game advantages in the engaging Hoppa! platform game, and the NFT Breeding Room for creating new Rabbit NFTs. Anticipate the upcoming release of Rabbit Island, where RBITS will enhance your virtual journey and contribute to the establishment of a vibrant Rabbit Economy.
Reward Distribution: A 2% portion of every trade seamlessly flows to holders, ensuring a continual stream of rewards. This means that the more RBITS you hold, the more you earn.
Auto-locking Liquidity: Automatically allocating 2% of each transaction to the liquidity pool, the resulting LP tokens are securely held by the contract address. This not only mitigates volatility but also contributes to an everstrengthening price floor.
Project Funding: Dedicating 1% of transactions to fund the project, alongside staking and liquidity, this strategic allocation significantly bolsters the development and enrichment of the RBITS ecosystem.
Initial High Tax Rate: Upon launch, a substantial tax rate of 25% is implemented, and within a 4-12 hour timeframe, the tax rate is manually adjusted to swiftly reach the target of 5% (allocated as 2% for rewards, 2% for liquidity, and 1% for project funds). This deliberate reduction is performed manually to optimize the protocol's performance. Additionally, the system incorporates a dynamic tax rate feature to counteract potential disruptions caused by rapid exits of significant token holders, commonly known as sniper whales.
100% Rugpull Safe: Dx.app ensures the secure locking of the initial LP tokens. As the contract generates additional LP tokens, withdrawals for project funds and marketing are allowed but are subject to limitations. Withdrawals are capped at a maximum of 10% of the newly generated LP tokens, and each withdrawal triggers an automatic 4-week time lock. This dual mechanism, combining both capping and time locking, not only guarantees equitable distribution, prioritizes token stability, and instills confidence within the community but also enhances rugpull safety by placing restrictions on withdrawals.

HOWTO BUY ON UNISWAP
1. Access Uniswap: Open the Uniswap App on your web browser. The official Uniswap interface is found at https://app.uniswap.org.
2. Connect Your Wallet: Connect your Ethereum wallet to Uniswap. Make sure your wallet is funded with enough ETH for the purchase.
3. Select the Token Pair: In the "From" field, choose ETH (Ethereum) as the currency you want to swap, and in the "To" field, select RBITS. If RBITS is not automatically listed, you may need to input the contract address to locate it.
4. Adjust Slippage Tolerance: Set the slippage tolerance based on the current market conditions. Slippage is the acceptable difference between the expected price and the executed price. Check our pinned messages in Telegram when in doubt.
5. Enter the Amount: Input the amount of ETH you want to swap for RBITS. The corresponding amount of RBITS will be calculated based on the current exchange rate.
6. Approve Token Spend: If it's your first time buying RBITS, you may need to approve the spending of your RBITS by interacting with your wallet.
7. Confirm and Swap: Review the transaction details and click 'Confirm' or 'Swap'. Confirm the transaction on your wallet interface when prompted.
8. Wait for Confirmation: Wait for the transaction to be confirmed on the Ethereum blockchain. This may take a few minutes, and you can track the progress on your wallet or through a blockchain explorer.
9. View Transaction on Etherscan: After the transaction is confirmed, you can view it on Etherscan by clicking the transaction link provided by Uniswap. This will show you the details of the transaction on the Ethereum blockchain.
Remember to use caution, ensure you are on the official Uniswap website and double-check the contract address to avoid scams. Additionally be aware of gas fees associated with Ethereum transactions.
